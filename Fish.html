Un Sustainability Goal 14
Fish

Fishing Sustainability

With the world's population growing the demand on the oceans fish stocks has increased significantly. Currently the demand on the ocean is unsustainable.
- One third of the assessed global fish stocks are over fished, and this proportion is steadily increasing. And since not all of the oceans fish stocks have been assessed, this figure may be underestimated.

- 3 Billion people rely on fish as a major source of animal protein creating a huge demand.

- 98 Million Tonnes of fish valued at 150 billion was caught in 2013.

- The demand on the ocean has been steadily increasing by 3-5 million tons each year since 2002.

- The amount of apex marine predators has disappeared by up to 90%.
- Fishing nets catch far more than intended including animals such as turtles, dolphins, sharks and sea birds.
- Bottom trawling is destroying the bottom of the ocean and is the equivalent of deforestatiwhalon.
